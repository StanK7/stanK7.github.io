Attractions

Taniti Island offers a variety of attractions to make your visit unforgettable. From natural wonders to exciting activities, there's something for everyone.

Beaches

Enjoy the white sandy beaches that encircle Yellow Leaf Bay.

Rainforest

Explore the lush tropical rainforests.

Volcano

Visit our active volcano for an unforgettable experience.

Local History Museum

Learn about the rich history of Taniti Island.

Charter Fishing Tours

Go on exciting fishing tours.

Snorkeling

Discover the underwater world with snorkeling adventures.

Zip-lining

Experience the thrill of zip-lining.

Helicopter Rides

Take a scenic helicopter ride over Taniti Island.

Art Galleries

Visit local art galleries showcasing Tanitian art.

Bowling

Enjoy a fun time at the bowling alley.

Dining Options

Restaurants

Taniti currently has 10 restaurants: five serve mostly local fish and rice, three serve American-style meals, and two serve Pan-Asian cuisine.

Grocery Stores

Convenience stores, supermarkets and grocery stores

Taniti has two supermarkets, two smaller grocery stores, and one convenience store that is open 24 hours a day.

Sightseeing and Tours

Taniti City and Yellow Leaf Bay

Explore Taniti City and the beautiful Yellow Leaf Bay.

Boat and Bus Tours

Take guided tours around the island by boat or bus.

Hiking in the Rainforest

Go on a hiking adventure in the lush rainforest.

Entertainment and Activities

Nightlife

Enjoy vibrant nightlife with pubs, a microbrewery, and a dance club.

Daytime Activities

Participate in various daytime activities like visiting art galleries and bowling.
